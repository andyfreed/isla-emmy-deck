# Gameplay Ideas — Isla & Emmy: Funky Islands (Deck)

Running scratchpad of ideas to tie together later. Nothing here is final.

## ⭐ Zodiac Powers (Emmy's idea)
Collect the 12 **Korean zodiac (띠)** animal signs throughout the game; each grants
an ability. This becomes the spine of the adventure: "collect all 12" is the reason
to explore every island. Fits the no-combat, no-lose, gentle design — all abilities
are about exploring/discovering/traversing, never fighting.

| Animal | Korean | Ability | Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rat | 쥐 | **Squeeze** — shrink to slip through tiny gaps to hidden rooms | active |
| Ox | 소 | **Strong** — push heavy boulders/blocks | active |
| Tiger | 호랑이 | **Pounce** — quick dash across gaps / zoom around | active |
| Rabbit | 토끼 | **Hop** — high double-jump over obstacles & to ledges | active |
| Dragon | 용 | **Glide** — float/hover over water & pits a few seconds | active |
| Snake | 뱀 | **Slither** — wiggle through narrow paths; calm hazards | active |
| Horse | 말 | **Gallop** — sustained super-run across open areas | active |
| Sheep | 양 | **Fluff** — bounce high, always land softly & safe | active |
| Monkey | 원숭이 | **Climb** — scale walls/trees, swing on vines | active |
| Rooster | 닭 | **Wake-Up** — reveal map, nearby hidden presents sparkle | passive |
| Dog | 개 | **Sniff** — puppy pal sniffs out & fetches hidden snacks | passive |
| Pig | 돼지 | **Lucky** — finds extra treats; snacks/presents worth more | passive |

### How to obtain
- Each island hides a **zodiac shrine** (or a baby animal you help) that grants its sign.
- Collecting all 12 = main goal / completion.

### How they work (kept simple for a 6yo, interesting for a 9yo)
- **Passive** signs just stack — world gets richer/easier as you collect (fits "older
  helps younger, no losing").
- **Active** signs are a power on a button; equip one at a time, swap at shrines.

### Personalization — signature starting signs (LOCKED)
Korean zodiac is by birth year, so each sister starts with her own real sign:
- **Isla (born 2020) → Rat 쥐 → Squeeze** (shrink into tiny gaps)
- **Emmy (born 2016) → Monkey 원숭이 → Climb** (scale walls, swing on vines)

Nice age-logic: big sister = climber, little sister = squeezer. Picking a hero
therefore plays differently from the first second.
(Caveat: zodiac flips at Lunar New Year ~late Jan/Feb; confirm if either was born
that early — otherwise Rat & Monkey stand.)

### Two heroes — DIRECTION: Final Fantasy party model (CHOSEN)
Both sisters are ALWAYS together as a party of two; both are equally part of the
story. Single-player: you directly steer the **lead** (the one picked at select),
the other **follows and fully participates**.
- **Swap lead with a button** → use either sister's zodiac power on demand
  (Emmy = Climb, Isla = Squeeze).
- Enables **two-sister puzzles**: Isla squeezes through a gap to open a gate for
  Emmy; Emmy climbs to drop a vine for Isla. (The co-op heart of the NES vision,
  inside a single-player game.)
- Each sister starts with her signature sign; the party collects all 12 through play.

**OPEN FORK — combat or not?** User referenced "fights" (FF framing). Decide:
- (a) No-combat: "fights" = friendly co-op challenges/events, no game-over (matches
  original kid-friendly design).
- (b) Gentle FF-style turn-based battles: both sisters take turns w/ zodiac powers,
  soft & un-scary (enemies give up / turn friendly, nobody gets hurt).
